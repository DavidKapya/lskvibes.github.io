1. Information We Collect

We collect information that is necessary to operate a secure, efficient, and feature-rich digital marketplace.

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

1.3 Transaction and Payment Information

  • Transaction Details: We collect details about transactions you conduct on the Platform, including the date, amount, and status of the transaction, as well as a reference ID.
  • In-App Purchase Information: When you make purchases through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store (e.g., for Premium Subscriptions, Product Sponsorships, or Contact Reveals), we receive information from them to confirm your purchase, such as a transaction receipt and your subscription status and expiry date.
